# Quick Reference Guide - Brand Marketing Skill

## Setup (One Time)

1. Copy `brand-profile-template.json` to create your brand profile
2. Fill in your brand details (or ask Claude to help)
3. Save it in your project as `[your-brand-name].json`

## Common Commands

### Content Generation

#### Social Media Posts
```
"Using [brand-name] profile, create 3 Instagram posts about [topic]"
"Generate a week of social media content across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter"
"Create Instagram Stories script about our new product launch"
"Write TikTok video ideas that match our brand voice"
```

#### Email Marketing
```
"Draft a welcome email for new subscribers using our brand profile"
"Create a product launch email sequence (3 emails)"
"Write a re-engagement email for customers who haven't purchased in 60 days"
"Generate a newsletter featuring our sustainability initiatives"
```

#### Ad Copy
```
"Create Google Search ads for [product/service]"
"Write Facebook ad variations (3 headlines, 3 descriptions) for A/B testing"
"Generate Instagram ad copy targeting [specific audience segment]"
"Create a video ad script for YouTube pre-roll"
```

#### Website Content
```
"Write a landing page for [product] highlighting our differentiators"
"Create product descriptions for our e-commerce site"
"Draft an About Us page using our brand story"
"Write conversion-focused homepage copy"
```

#### Blog & Long-form
```
"Outline a blog post about [topic] incorporating our core values"

"Create a case study template for customer success stories"
"Draft a press release for [announcement]"
```

### Brand Consistency

### Campaign Development


## Common Workflows

### Launching a New Product
1. "Create messaging framework for [product] launch"
2. "Generate email sequence (announcement, education, last chance)"
3. "Create social media content calendar for 2-week launch"
4. "Write ad copy for paid campaigns"
5. "Draft press release and media pitch"

### Monthly Content Planning
1. "Review upcoming events/seasonality relevant to our brand"
2. "Create content calendar across all channels"
3. "Generate specific content for each pillar"
4. "Plan engagement tactics and community initiatives"
5. "Schedule review points for performance"

### Campaign Development
1. "Define campaign objective and target audience segment"
2. "Develop key messages aligned with brand profile"
3. "Create channel-specific content"
4. "Generate creative variations for A/B testing"
5. "Plan measurement and optimization strategy"

## Troubleshooting

**Content doesn't sound like our brand?**
- Ensure brand voice section is detailed and specific
- Provide examples of existing content you love
- Reference specific tone attributes in your request

**Missing key differentiators?**
- Check that your offers section is complete
- Explicitly request: "emphasize our differentiators"
- Update profile with competitive wins

**Not addressing audience pain points?**
- Review and expand target audience section
- Specifically ask to address pain points in request
- Test content with actual customers for resonance

**Too generic?**
- Add more specific brand story details
- Include unique phrases or language your brand uses
- Provide examples of campaigns you admire

---

**Remember:** The more detailed and specific your brand profile, the more on-brand and effective your generated content will be. Keep it updated and iterate based on what works!
